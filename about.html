About Victoria Sports Academy

Established in 2016, Victoria Sports is a premier sports academy committed to nurturing athletic excellence and promoting a healthy, active lifestyle. Located in the vibrant University City at Skyline University College, Sharjah, we offer a dynamic range of services—from elite sports education to facility management, rental services, and international programs.

Our 26-acre, state-of-the-art sports facility reflects our unwavering commitment to developing world-class athletes across cricket, football, and more.

Our Vision

To build a generation of healthy, confident, and proficient athletes equipped with the technical skills, mindset, and discipline to succeed—on and off the field.

Our Mission

To deliver impactful sports education and physical training beyond the classroom, cultivating leadership, discipline, teamwork, and personal growth through high-quality experiences.
